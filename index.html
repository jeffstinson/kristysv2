A Local Landmark

Serving Enola Since 1958

Kristy’s Whistle Stop has been part of Enola for generations, welcoming families, regulars, and summer visitors with a classic roadside stand feel.

With its spot near the historic rail yards and its longtime hometown following, it is the kind of place people come back to year after year.
